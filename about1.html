About college

RR Institutions, established in 1993 by PKMET, has been providing various courses in Engineering, Management, Nursing, Pharmacy, Polytechnic, Architecture, Pre-University, Education, Allied and Para-Medical Sciences for 27 years. The institutions under RR institutions are approved by AICTE, UGC, INC, PCI, and the Government of Karnataka. RR Institutions is situated on a sprawling 27-acre green campus with a pleasant atmosphere and is easily accessible by road, rail, metro, and air. The institution is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure that creates a conducive environment for exploring higher learning, personal growth, and self-improvement. RR Institutions is committed to values such as innovation, dedication, enthusiasm, and honesty. To ensure excellent outcomes, the institution has an internal quality delivery monitoring mechanism and highly qualified, committed, and experienced teaching staff who will guide every student on their exploration, enrichment, and personal growth journey.
